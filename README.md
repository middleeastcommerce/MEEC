# Middle East E-Commerce (MEEC)

The **Middle East E-Commerce (MEEC)** token is the official digital asset powering the **Middle East Commerce** ecosystem—a next-generation digital marketplace connecting businesses and users across the Middle East and globally.

It is designed to enable fast, transparent, and secure transactions across e-commerce, services, advertising, and platform utilities.

The MEEC token is fully integrated into the Middle East Commerce ecosystem:

* **E-Commerce Payments:** Seamless checkout for goods.
* **Advertising & Promotions:** Boost visibility within the marketplace.
* **Store Subscriptions:** Pay for premium vendor tools.
